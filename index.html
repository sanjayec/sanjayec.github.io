Narrative Visualization of Automobile data

Convey how different types of automobiles spread across different drive-wheels and makers in the market and how sedan type models are available across all makers. After understanding the variety of vehicles in the market, How different types of automobiles influences the price of vehicle. At the end, make user understand the mileage of each engine type and understand that ratio of city-mileage and highway-mileage does not really vary much across engine-types.

Categorization based on hierarchy Bodystyle, DriveWheels, Make

Zoomable sunburst displays the count of vehicles in different categories in a hierarchical way.

Total count is shown at the center, then divided based on body styles, then by driveWheels and then by Make.

Mouseover on each region gives the count and category. Click on each region to drilldown further.

Average Price vs Make, Body style

Stacked bar chart displays the Average price distribution across body-styles for each make.

Price is shown on y-axis, Make on x-axis. Body style is integrated inside each bar.

Mouseover gives the details about each particular group.

Price Analysis

Scatter plot shows price variation based on different parameters. Price is on y-axis, Drive-wheels are shown using color.

Select the x-axis of interest to do price analysis

Legend can be clicked to highlight the data of that particular group

Mouseover on each bubble gives more details for that data point.

Select X-Axis measure

Average Miles Per Gallon per Engine Type

Average MPG is shown per each engine-type in bar chart and also for each engine-type how city-mpg and highway-mpg varies can be seen in pie chart.

Mouse over each bar to see the triggered city-mpg/highway-mpg division.

Mouse over each angle of pie-chart to see the corresponding data in bar chart.
